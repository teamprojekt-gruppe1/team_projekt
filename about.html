De Studerendes Råd (DSR)

Hvem er vi?

Studenterdemokratiet og det studenterpolitiske arbejde prioriteres højt på KEA. DSR sikrer en løbende dialog mellem KEAs ledelse og de studerende, med henblik på at skabe et inkluderende studiemiljø med plads til alle, samt højne kvaliteten af vores uddannelser. DSR repræsenterer og varetager de studerendes interesser af både faglig og social karakter, og de arbejder for, at de studerende har en direkte indflydelse. DSR arbejder blandt andet for undervisning og det faglige udbytte relateret til uddannelserne samt tilrettelæggelse af undervisningen. Derudover har de en social opgave, der handler om at være en medvirkende faktor i etableringen af et trygt og inkluderende studiemiljø - både på de enkelte uddannelser, og på tværs af KEAs programområder. Dette gøres i samarbejde med tutor-teamsne og fredagsbarerne, som begge drives af frivilligt engagerede studerende.

Who are we?

The student democracy and student political work are highly prioritized at KEA. DSR ensures an ongoing dialogue between KEA's management and the students, with the aim of creating an inclusive study environment with space for everyone, as well as enhancing the quality of our education. DSR represents and safeguards the interests of the students in both academic and social aspects, and they work to ensure that students have direct influence. DSR works, among other things, on teaching and the academic benefits related to the programs, as well as the organization of the teaching. In addition, they have a social role, contributing to the establishment of a safe and inclusive study environment – both within individual programs and across KEA's areas of study. This is done in collaboration with tutor teams and the Friday bars, both of which are run by voluntarily engaged students.
